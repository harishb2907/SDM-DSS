About Placify

At Placify, we believe every student deserves a smooth and empowering transition from campus to career. Our platform is designed to simplify the placement process by providing students, faculty, and recruiters with powerful, user-friendly tools. From real-time eligibility checking to seamless application tracking, we aim to bridge the gap between student potential and industry opportunities. With advanced features, robust security, and a student-first mindset, Placify is here to fuel your journey toward success.

Why Choose Placify?

Student-First Design: Built specifically for students to make placement easy, transparent, and accessible.
Smart Eligibility Checking: Instantly see where you qualify based on your CGPA, skills, internships, and certifications.
Seamless Company Management: Organized placement drives and better communication with recruiters.
Real-Time Updates: Stay informed about upcoming placements, deadlines, and eligibility changes.
Secure and Reliable: Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) ensures your data stays safe.
Insightful Dashboards: Personalized dashboards for students, faculty, and coordinators.
Fast and Easy to Use: Lightweight, responsive, and designed for a stress-free experience.
Supportive Community: Continuous support to help students achieve placement success.

Our Services:

Profile Management: Students create and manage placement-ready profiles; admins manage student records.
Eligibility Filtering: Students check real-time eligibility for placements based on CGPA and skills, admins filter students for company shortlisting.
Placement Applications: Students apply to companies and track their applications; admins oversee application statuses.
Company & Drive Management: Admins manage company profiles, job roles, and placement drives; students apply for opportunities.
Analytics Dashboard: Both students and admins access real-time statistics and insights into placements, applications, and results.
Real-Time Updates: Students receive alerts on upcoming placements and deadlines; admins send notifications to students.
Secure Access: Multi-factor authentication ensures secure login and data protection for both students and admins.
